My name is Ryan Colosanti. I am an aspiring game designer currently based in between Richmond, Virginia and Williamsburg, Virginia. I have always had a passion for video games from the first time I played Pokemon Blue on the Gameboy Color. They have always been a way for me to relax and escape for a brief period of time. I love being a part of the many different worlds and discovering all of their secrets. Now I want to give that same feeling adventure and wonder to other people of all walks of life by creating the same video games that make me excited to pick up a controller and turn on the console.

Here, you will find a multitude of projects I have completed in the past six months during a coding boot camp. These projects were made with real professional work and in-demand skills in mind. While these projects are focus on web development, I am also spending my personal time learning how to design and write code for video games.

Before I chose a path in development, I graduated from the University of Colorado with a Bachelor of Arts in History. What draws me to history is the storytelling aspect.
